Frieren

About Frieren

Frieren is an elven mage who has lived for over a thousand years and defeated the Demon King with her former group. She is currently accompanied by her human apprentice, Fern.

Frieren and her former group

Frieren's Characteristics

  1. She carries a large red staff and many trinkets with unique spells
  2. Much of her magical aura is hidden to have enemies underestimate her skills
  3. After traveling with her last group, she was known as Frieren the Slayer
